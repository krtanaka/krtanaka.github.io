Kisei R. Tanaka, Ph.D.

I am a research marine biologist at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (Honolulu, HI). My research interests include impacts of climate on living marine resources, fisheries stock assessments, spatiotemporal models, and the use of multi-annual to decadal environmental predictions for marine resource applications.
