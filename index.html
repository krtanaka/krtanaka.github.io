Research Marine Biologist at NOAA: National Oceanic & Atmospheric Administration

I am a research marine biologist at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (Honolulu, HI). My research interests include impacts of climate on living marine resources, fisheries stock assessments, spatiotemporal models, and use of multi-annual to decadal environmental predictions for marine resource applications.
