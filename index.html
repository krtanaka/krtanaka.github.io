Kisei R. Tanaka, Ph.D.

田中貴生(博士)、海洋生物学者、アメリカ海洋大気庁

I am a research marine biologist at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (Honolulu, HI). My research interests include how environmental factors impact marine life and use long-term environmental data for marine applications.

I have master’s degrees in Environmental Policy and Environmental Science from American University and a Ph.D. in Ecology and Environmental Sciences from the University of Maine. Before joining NOAA in 2020, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Princeton University and worked with UNEP, the Antarctic and Southern Ocean Coalition, and the Monterey Bay Aquarium.
