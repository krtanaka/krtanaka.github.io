Kisei R. Tanaka, Ph.D.

田中貴生(博士)、海洋生物学者、アメリカ海洋大気庁

I am a research marine biologist at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (Honolulu, HI). My research interests include impacts of climate on living marine resources, spatiotemporal models, and the use of multi-annual to decadal environmental predictions for marine resource applications.
